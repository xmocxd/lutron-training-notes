--- LEGACY PANELS ---
=====================

legacy panel interface - only part of an athena system - allows integration with legacy panels

circuit selector for quantum / used with legacy panel interface for athena


--- GP Panel ---

pre assembled panel with dimming cards
wires like a distribution panel
from 3 output to 144 output depending on model

--- LP Panel ---

has modules inside (4U modules), each module has 4 outputs -- shares 1 feed between its 4 outputs
ONLY for forward phase dimming - phase controlled loads

--- XP Panel ---

Switching panel, pre-assembled with non-dim modules

--- CCP Panel ---

Custom configured panel
Can put different types of modules inside

NOTE: CANNOT field modify the types or numbers of modules - if they do, they have to have another inspection done on the panel

--- circuit selectors ---
(only works with quantum)
1 or 2 link models
link A
link B -- used as a DMX link


--- Legacy Panel Interface ---
(only works with athena)
converts the panel from a power panel link to a QS-Link
LED diagnostics / Local control buttons
Supports emergency lighting applications

--- modules ---
1 feed shared between x outputs:
1U - 1 circuit
2U - 2 circuit
4U - 4 circuit
4A - phase adapative module 4 circuit
the rotary dial sets which module number it is when installed

4M - motor module - four circuit motor module -- used for motorized hurricane shutters , gates, etc -- every output has 2 sets of controls for 2-way motors / open/close

XP/XP2 - switching module (4 circuit) --- the XP2 module only goes into a CCP (custom) panel

GP Harrier Card -- goes into a GP panel - 120V and 277V models

TVM -- 0-10V module -- either GRX-TVM-ISO2 (POWER MODULE only), or GRX-TVM2 (ballast control module) -- need one power module to power multiple TVM2s

--- CONTROL HARNESS ---

all modules are pre-addressed from the factory
4 pin connectors to connect the harness

GP / Harrier cards use a 12 pin connected and are pre-wired from the factory
green LED = 120V card 
amber LED = 277V card

277V cards can be used in either panel type

TVM - 0-10 wiring is polarity sensitive, must be landed on the correct terminals

we should never be pulling out jumpers LIVE


--- BCI-0-10 (obsolete) ---

allows control of 3-wire from a 0-10V source
replaced by the phase controlled ESN -- previously ESNs did not do phase adapative version

connects to a PHPM-WBX


--- TVI-LMF-2A ---

allows you to use an ecosystem control system to control 0-10V loads
suggested for SINGLE FIXTURE control -- 2 amps of switching load


--- C5-XPJ-16A ---

EcoSystem input to SWITCHING output
control up to 16A of lighting load
any type of switched load + also controls motor loads

has wired sensor inputs -- provides 20VDC power out to wired sensors

uses 1 eco address


--- C5-BMJ-16A ---

EcoSystem input to 3-wire output
also provide wired sensor connections


--- C5-BMF-2A ---

this product has made a resurgence -- can be used to add connections for wired sensors to LED drivers

upgrade system from ballast to LED

only rated for 2A

usually just used as a sensor input, not usually used to control a fixture
