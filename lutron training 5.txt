- anything else i need to know?

WHEN YOU GET A WORK ORDER
--------

1. GET DB - Check out the work order, check out the asset, get the database from the asset
--- QUANTUM -> it will be under related -> files
--- Athena -> it will be link under special instructions - when you click nothing happens but it will show up in designer
--- Vive -> NO DB

2. Call job site contact
--- Go to functions, scroll down to checklist - select the checklist
- Startup readiness - quantum / athena
- can go through the checklist while you are on the phone

***WHEN YOU GET TO THE JOB***

3. Go to service order and --- under FUNCTIONS -> Check in
4. When done same -> Check out
5. Add your timesheet entries --- under FUNCTIONS


***WHEN DONE WITH JOB***

6. Do the trip report --- under WORK ORDER -> RELATED -> Trip reports (CHECK IN creates the trip report links)
--- SOMETIMES DUPLICATES -- it will have 0 hours - change to 1 and submit for approval - say delete
7. After final checkout, go into ASSET and upload the QUANTUM database -- QUANTUM only
8. Change job status to completed -- under RELATED check work order line item to complete or whatever status it is
9. Click create service report
10. Click submit for approval
11. Go back out --- under FUNCTIONS - click service report and click send
